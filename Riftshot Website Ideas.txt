Welcome to <st>Stargrove</st> STARV Island!

This is Planet Thera, 20XX. This is a world of science and love, much like our own, but with command over forces we've only barely begun to scratch. Auroras manifest once a year. Robotics have advanced to the point of defying physics. And yet, even in this wonderous world, greed and doubt still persist. The multi-industry monopoly STARV Inc. has has taken command of the unassuming Stargrove Island, a place harboring a deep secret. Reality is tearing at the seams. Festivals are self-destructing. And the island's aurora shards are being gathered for a sinister purpose.
Until a Shooting Star appears.

Use your slingshot to shoot across the sky and into enemies as your make your way across open lands, collecting Aurora Shards to help repair the island!

10 compact and dense areas, ranging from a city perpetually bathed in neon, to a desert stuck in eternal thunderstorms. Uncover the secrets and weirdness in every corner of the island. 

A strange, heartfelt story about the power of creation and connection. It's goofy, it's genuine, it's got a Time Being. Wanna know what that is? Me too. 


To save the world, you must create a safe world for others. Will you bridge that space?
