Welcome to Stargrove Island!

This is Planet Thera, 20XX. A world of science and love, much like our own, but with command over forces we've only barely begun to scratch. Auroras manifest once a year. Robotics have advanced to the point of defying physics. And yet, even in this wonderous world, greed and doubt still persist. The multi-industry monopoly STARV Inc. has has taken command of the unassuming Stargrove Island, a place harboring a deep, reality-shattering secret. People are shuddered inside their homes. Reality is tearing at the seams. And the island's aurora shards are being gathered for a sinister purpose.

Until a Shooting Star appears.
